The Eternal Student


Reflection

I am currently a computer science student, studying at Salt Lake City Community College.
This is my first web development class. I knew nothing about H.T.M.L. going into C.S.I.S. 1430
but it was really interesting to learn honestly. I was surprised at the amount of creativity
it required to make web site. That one was difficult for at first. I had to learn to think
like a user using my web site, while learning to think like a programmer. I have learned to
break the problem down to it individual elements and work it through piece by piece until you
identify the issue. This actually helped me when it came to math as well. Working algebra equations
and debugging a web page have a lot of similarities in the way you approach them, so building that
skill in web design has helped me grow in my math skill, and it was all because of this class.
I would recommend taking this class to anyone because i feel it is vitally important to at least
have a basic knowledge of how the Internet works since it plays such a massive part in our lives.
I now feel confident that this is what i want to do as a career and i am honestly excited to take
the next class during the summer.
