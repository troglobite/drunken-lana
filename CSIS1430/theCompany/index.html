What do we offer?


Custom Frames

If you have the dream we have the tools, Or if appreciate our dreams we can build you a one off bike. We built show bikes all the way to building ultitie bikes meant to move large ojects. No idea is a bad idea and if you can dream it then we can most definately build it. Prices are per case, but our hourly rate is $35 an hour spent, but rest assured when we are on the job an well spent.

PRE-BUILT BEAUTIES

Our Aviato line has won the Gold Sproket at the 2015 International Bicycle Builders Conference. They capture the Beauty of the Italian hand folded cromoly-steel tubing. The design is styled after the Italian Fighter planes of WWII. Light and responsive handling makes you swear your leaving the ground behind.

PRE-LOVED AND RECYCLED

We also buy and sell used bikes. All bikes go through a multi point inspection and a tune up to make sure they get the TLC they need before hitting our show room floor. They are ready to carve up some pavement the second you buy them. Think of them as last years Kentucky Derby winners, all the pedigree at penny's on the dollar. Not to mention saving a bike from the junk yard is a noble thing to do.

REPAIR

Is your bike not feeling right. Bikes get old and they need repair. A little TLC goes along way and offer very competetive prices. Repair work costs $25 an hour but with our expertise that turns into big savings for you, and your cycle will get the care it needs.
